# VisualNovel_MechanicalHeart

Hi everyone!
This is version 1.0.9 of my visual novel "Mechanical Heart".

Implemented: TextArchitect, DialogueController, CharacterController, CommandController, AudioController, and graphic panel management.

# Setting

The story unfolds in a steampunk world set in 19th century England, where the main working class consists of mechanics. Instead of electricity, the world has embraced mechanisms as the basis of its progress. In addition to them, there is a lower caste of religious fanatics called astronomers, who are solely focused on studying the night sky and seeking answers about the origins of life and extraterrestrial civilizations. Some astronomers worship a mysterious substance known as the "Sky" and believe that their ancestors were able to make contracts with it to obtain limitless knowledge and wisdom.

At the center of the story is the main heroine, the daughter of the country's most renowned clockmaker, the trusted representative of the pontiff. Although this is prohibited by law, she is the legal heir of her father's business, and also studies at the university.. However, a crucial question arises: does she truly desire this path? What does her soul and heart long for, and what will ultimately prevail – rational pragmatism or the dreamy heart that yearns for the stars?
